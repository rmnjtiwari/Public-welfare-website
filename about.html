About Public Welfare Society

History

  • 1998: Public Welfare society was founded.
  • 2002: Public Welfare society first outreach centre in Mumbai was founded.
  • 2005: Camp Model was introduced to take the skill development courses to rural interiors and tribal belts.
  • 2009: Another crucial pillar of YP - Partnerships was introduced to work with like-minded NGOs and Institutes.
  • 2017: Mobile Operation App launched and rollout for entire field staff for real time capture of dada.

Who we are

With a firm belief in giving a SECOND CHANCE we are here to make a difference to the society and strive for a better tomorrow and a better India. Together, we are Public welfare Society.

Vision

Create opportunities for school dropouts and deprived youth to help them lead productive and socially useful lives.

Mission

To give a second chance to the less educated deprived youth through wage or self-employment based on urban and rural livelihood training provided in partnership with stakeholders.
